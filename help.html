Welcome to Jaayma Help Center

Find step-by-step guides for buyers, sellers, and deliverers

Buyers Help

How to create a package?

Learn how to create and customize your delivery packages

  1. Log into your Jaayma buyer account
  2. Click on "Create Package" from your dashboard
  3. Fill in package details (dimensions, weight, fragility)
  4. Add pickup and delivery addresses
  5. Set your preferred delivery timeframe
  6. Review and save your package information

How to place an Order?

Step-by-step guide to placing your delivery orders

  1. Select your created package or create a new one
  2. Choose your delivery speed (standard, express, or urgent)
  3. Browse available deliverers and their ratings
  4. Select your preferred deliverer
  5. Review order details and pricing
  6. Confirm and submit your order

How to perform payments?

Secure payment methods and transaction guides

  1. Go to your order summary page
  2. Select your preferred payment method (card, mobile money, bank transfer)
  3. Enter payment details securely
  4. Review the total amount and fees
  5. Confirm payment authorization
  6. Receive payment confirmation and order tracking details

Sellers Help

How to add new items?

Add and manage your inventory on Jaayma

  1. Access your seller dashboard
  2. Click "Add New Item" or "Add Product"
  3. Upload high-quality product images
  4. Fill in product details (name, description, category)
  5. Set pricing and inventory quantities
  6. Add shipping information and publish item

How to manage Orders?

Track and manage your customer orders

  1. Navigate to "Orders" section in your dashboard
  2. View incoming orders and their status
  3. Accept or decline orders based on availability
  4. Update order status (processing, packed, shipped)
  5. Communicate with buyers through messaging system
  6. Mark orders as completed once delivered

How to get upload authorizations?

Verify your account for selling privileges

  1. Go to "Account Verification" in settings
  2. Upload government-issued ID document
  3. Provide business registration documents (if applicable)
  4. Submit tax identification number
  5. Wait for document review (1-3 business days)
  6. Receive verification confirmation and selling access

How to get Paid?

Set up payments and withdraw earnings

  1. Complete account verification process
  2. Add bank account or mobile money details
  3. Set up automatic or manual payout preferences
  4. Complete orders and wait for buyer confirmation
  5. Funds released after successful delivery (2-7 days)
  6. Request withdrawal or receive automatic payout

Deliverers Help

How to apply to orders?

Find and apply for delivery opportunities

  1. Open the deliverer dashboard
  2. Browse available delivery orders in your area
  3. Check order details (distance, payment, timeline)
  4. Submit your delivery proposal with competitive rates
  5. Wait for buyer selection and approval
  6. Accept the order and begin delivery process

How to perform Order Deliveries?

Complete deliveries safely and efficiently

  1. Confirm order acceptance and review pickup details
  2. Navigate to pickup location using GPS
  3. Verify package details and take photos
  4. Update status to "Package Collected"
  5. Deliver to destination and confirm with recipient
  6. Complete delivery confirmation and photo proof

How to get Paid?

Payment process for completed deliveries

  1. Complete delivery and get buyer confirmation
  2. Submit delivery completion report
  3. Wait for payment processing (24-48 hours)
  4. Check earnings in your deliverer wallet
  5. Request payout to your preferred payment method
  6. Receive payment within 1-3 business days
